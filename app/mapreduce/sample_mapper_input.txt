1	Domestic_Cat_Overview	Cats are small, carnivorous mammals that are often kept as house pets. Domestic cats have been living with humans for thousands of years and are one of the most popular pets worldwide. They are known for their agility, independence, and hunting abilities.
2	Cat_Behavior_And_Communication	Cats communicate through vocalizations like meowing, purring, and hissing. They also use body language including tail positions and ear movements to express their mood. Cats are territorial animals and often mark their territory with scent from special glands on their face and paws.
3	Popular_Cat_Breeds	The Maine Coon is one of the largest domestic cat breeds known for its distinctive physical appearance. Siamese cats are recognizable by their color points and blue eyes, while Persian cats have long fur and flat faces. The Ragdoll is known for going limp when picked up, and the Bengal resembles a small leopard.
